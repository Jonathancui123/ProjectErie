How It Works

1. Crowd-Sourced Pings
Trash-spotters conveniently ping the location of sighted trash on and around Lake using our SMS service
2. Data Analysis
Data points are evaluated into a dynamic lake-health score and presented as a map of recently reported trash.
3. Trash Management
Using the data, vigilante trash-hunters can retrieve and dispose of trash while local authorities are empowered to make informed policies.
