Our Simple SMS Ping System

How to report via SMS

Step 1:

Spot the trash

Step 2:

Text ProjectErie at +1-647-559-1371 and use the keyword 'report' followed by the 'type', 'severity' and 'location' properties each separated by a space

Step 3:

Our server accepts the following options for the properties: "report (trash/algae) (LOW/MID/HIGH) ±#.##,±#.##"

e.g. report trash mid 7.38,-2.21

How to Check for Nearby Trash

Step 1:

Text ProjectErie at +1-647-559-1371 with format "query [coordinates] [search_radius]"

Step 2:

Receive a text containing the closest recent report within your search radius in the format "[distance] [coordinates] [type] [severity]"

Step 3:

Happy Hunting!
